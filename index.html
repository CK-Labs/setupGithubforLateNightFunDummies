Set up a personal Github Page for Fun Dummies who are trying to escape fumigation late at night in the office

Step 1. Create a Github account.
Click on the signup button and follow instructions (such as creating a username, entering and confirming your email, creating a password and verifying your email! Tadaa!
You now are a proud owner of a Github account.)

Step 2. Sip your tea. Play chill music
Click on your profile picture on the upper right hand corner 'Your Profile', select Repositories and click on 'New'.

Step 3. Enter a name for your website on Github.

Step 3.5 At the bottom, press "Create Repository" button.

Step 4. Hold your breath.

Step 5. You will see 2 options - but I only know how to upload an existing file on Github. So let's do that! Wooo~

Step 6. (still with me?) I can see you!

Step 7. When you choose to Upload an Existing File, you will land on a page that allows the good ol' drag-and-drop.
Drag and Drop files + press the commit button.

Step 8. Test your site! Visit the site entered in Step 3 in a browser (we know you can do it!). [name].github.io
